Terms of Service

1. Subscriptions & Payment

  • Weekly Sub: £3 per player, per session
  • Bank Transfer: Pay a few days before the game to guarantee your spot
  • Cash: Pay on the day — but if spots are full, you may not be able to play
  • First come, first served
  • Once all spots are filled, we'll switch to a reverse sub system (details to follow)

2. Refunds

  • No refunds if you pay your £3 sub but fail to show up
  • If you pay and can't make it, your spot is forfeited — no money back
  • If we cancel a session for any reason, you'll receive a full refund

3. Attendance

  • Turn up and pay to secure your place
  • Once spots are full, no further players can join that session
  • If you're running late, message us — we may be able to hold your spot

4. Conduct

  • Respect all players, regardless of ability
  • No aggressive behaviour or foul language
  • We play for fun — enjoy the game!
  • The organiser reserves the right to ask anyone to leave

5. Playing at Your Own Risk

  • All players participate at their own risk
  • Mighty Men FC accepts no liability for injuries
  • Please ensure you are fit to play

6. Changes to Terms

We reserve the right to change these terms at any time. Any changes will be posted on this page.
